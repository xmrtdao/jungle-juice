Our Story

Born from the Rainforest

Nestled at the base of Volcán Arenal, Jungle Juice is a love letter to Costa Rica's wild abundance. Every smoothie starts with fruit picked at sunrise from the fertile slopes surrounding La Fortuna — mangoes blushed with volcanic minerals, papayas sweetened by mountain mist, and coffee grown in the shade of ancient trees.

We believe the best things in life are uncomplicated. No powders, no shortcuts. Just the pure, vibrant taste of the jungle, blended into every cup.
